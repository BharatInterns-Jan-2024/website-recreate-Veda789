Unposted Letters

Vimal Chandran , first published his illustration series ‘Unposted letters’ on social media in the early 2011 and it became hugely popular among online community. The polka dot umbrella and the tiny bird became iconic when the story unveiled in a truly unique style with bright dreamy colours and surreal atmosphere. This is the first edition book with 46 of his beautiful soulful illustrations from the series.
